We build websites.
That's the whole job.

A small Melbourne studio that hand-codes custom websites for Australian small businesses, then looks after them for as long as you want us to. No templates, no platforms to learn, no handover document to bury in a drawer.

The approach

Every Clickmax site starts as an empty file. We write the HTML, the CSS, the JavaScript — the whole thing, tuned to your brand and your business. No themes, no page builders, no recycled layouts.

It takes longer than dragging blocks around in a site builder. The result is a site that loads faster, scores better on Google, and doesn't look like everyone else's.

The ongoing bit

After launch, your site stays with us. You message us when you need something changed, added, or fixed — we handle it. Most small updates go live the same day. Bigger work (a new section, a redesigned page) gets a quick scope and a price before we start.

You never log into a CMS. You never renew a plugin. You never get an email about a broken build. That's the deal.

The honest bit

We're not a 50-person agency. There's no account manager, no project tracker, no standing Tuesday call. If you message us on a Wednesday afternoon, a human reads it and replies. That's what "fully managed" actually means.

It also means we don't take on more clients than we can properly look after. When the calendar's full, we say so.
