Got any questions?

The stuff people usually want to know before getting started.

Do I have to start paying the monthly right away?

Nope. Every build comes with your first month of hosting and edits included — so once your site goes live, you've got 30 days to see how the service works before the monthly kicks in. If you decide it's not for you, no hard feelings.

Can I just buy a site build and handle the rest myself?

Yes. If you'd rather just pay the one-off build fee and take it from there, that's totally fine — we'll hand you the code, help you get your domain pointed at your own hosting, and you're off. Just a heads up though: you'll be on your own for hosting, security, edits, and anything that breaks. Most people find the monthly easier (and cheaper than hiring someone each time they need a change) — but the choice is yours.

What counts as a "small edit"?

Changing text, swapping photos, updating your hours or prices, adding a simple new page like an about or contact, tweaking colours — things like that. If you can describe it in a sentence or two, it's a small edit. Both plans include them with no extra cost.

What if I want to add a whole new page or section later?

Depends what it is. A simple page with some text and photos is usually fine under your plan, no extra cost. Something trickier — a booking system, a custom tool, a new integration — we'd work out a fair price with you first. No surprise bills.

Do I actually own my site?

Yep. Once the site is built and your domain is paid for, it's yours. If you ever decide to leave, we'll hand over the code and transfer the domain to you. No holding anything hostage.

How fast do edits actually happen?

Same business day, always. In practice, most things are done within the hour of you messaging us. Pro clients jump the queue if something's urgent.

Is there a cancellation fee?

No. If you want to cancel, just tell us. No fee, no notice period, no guilt trip. We'd rather earn your next month than trap you into it.

What if my business grows and I need more?

We'll sort it. Tell us what you need and we'll put together something that works — whether it's a few more pages, a custom tool, an online store, or a new integration. You won't outgrow us.

What about SEO?

Every site we build has the SEO basics done right in the code — fast loading, clean structure, proper meta tags, alt text, sitemap, all the stuff Google actually cares about. Pro plans go further: every few months we check in, look at what's working, tune the content, and add landing pages if you're going after something specific.

Why the upfront fee?

Because building a site properly takes real work — design, coding, testing, getting it live. The upfront covers that. The monthly covers everything after: hosting, edits, support, keeping it running. Two costs, both clear, nothing sneaky.
